Our Approach

Discover how our proven three-part strategy helps businesses like yours stand out, connect deeply with your audience, and drive lasting success.

Deep Dive Conversation

First and most importantly is identifying the need for our services, and if our companies are a good fit for each other. Once we have identified the opportunity to work together, and the most efficient starting point. We can schedule a DEEP DIVE conversation to create a strategy.

The Deep Dive conversation is where we will sit down for a formal meeting and learn everything about your sales, marketing and business process and devise the initial strategy to move forward with. This meeting usually lasts at least 90 minutes and will cover an overview of your entire business model.

Things we will need to cover, but not limited to:

  • Your current sales funnel process
  • Your industry overview
  • Your ideal customer avatar
  • Your product line
  • Profit margins
  • Marketing Budget
  • Sales conversion numbers
  • Marketing traffic numbers
  • Success and struggles
  • Goals
  • Our process and what to expect

Getting Found (SEO)

We could create the greatest video ever made for your business, however, if your audience is not finding your company, nobody will ever see it. Making sure we are reaching the consumer you’re trying to reach is essential.

By combining our proven strategy of Business profile management, website coding and content and specific location pages we can drastically improve your visibility by your ideal customers and in the locations you want to do more business. We do this with:

Website Content

The content of a website page tells the search engines what the page is about. Once the top phrases, content is written which targets the top phrases for the page and is consistent with the tone of the existing language of the website. Completion of work takes approximately 30 days from start to finish - with the website rankings for the pages improving within 7-14 days after the completion of work.

Website Coding

The coding of a website page indicates the topic of the content within it. The meta title and meta description are what actually appear in a search engine result and can determine if a potential new client clicks through to your website. Our coding program includes keyword research to identify top phrases to target. Once the top phrases are identified, all meta data and H tags are completed with the keyword phrases. Completion of work takes approximately 30 days from start to finish - with the website rankings for the pages improving within 7-14 days after the completion of work.

Local SEO Program

Local listing optimization includes local search engine optimization on Google, Bing, Yahoo!, Yelp, YP.com, Apple IOS, and Amazon Alexa – over 70 total online directories - as well as Google Business Profile ongoing optimization. The local seo program includes:

  • Ongoing Google My Business profile optimization
  • Weekly posting in the Google Business Profile (averaging 4 times per month)
  • Directory profile/listing management on over 70 of the leading online directories
  • Custom Google Review link creation

Location Pages

Additional location specific pages are created and added to the website – with each page dedicated to a geographic city or town the client services. The creation of the landing pages drastically improves organic search positioning related to the content on each page - top phrases and geographic area targeted. Landing pages are often a great solution for city neighborhoods or suburban towns that a business wants to target outside of the city or town the business is located in. Completion of work takes approximately 30 days from start to finish - with the website rankings for the pages improving within 7-14 days after the completion of work.

Strategic Video Assets

Once we have a working knowledge of your business and we are sure you are in front of your ideal customer, we can identify the most strategic use of video assets to guide these customers through your sales funnel.

Developing your sales funnel with videos

Every potential customer that is interested in the product or service you offer, who is out there researching a possible purchase, has a conversation going on in their head. Our strategy involves creating the right video content to insert a strategic message into that internal conversation. This message can be an FAQ video answering your most popular questions, it could address financing or affordability questions, or simply just assuring them that there’s a local business that will handle their purchase from start to finish without a worry.

  • Where is the best place for specific videos?
    • Top of the sales funnel
      • “A message for the masses” This is where we might create a general video aimed to drive more traffic to a website or increase social media engagement and would not have messaging that is too specific. Who you are, what your company does, what areas you service etc. We want as many people as possible to be aware of these things and engage this video to begin their journey down your sales funnel.
    • Middle of the sales funnel
      • This is where the number of people dwindles from the top of your funnel, where we were just appealing to the masses, down to the people that are actually in the market for your product or service. This can be a message setting you apart from your competition or it can be content that separates viewers into different funnels, or even a video that goes into more depth on your product or service reinforcing you as an industry expert. The type of video inserted here is limitless, and will always be specific to your business model.
    • Bottom of the sales funnel
      • The videos at the bottom of most sales funnels will generally be much more topic specific. The ease of process or the re-emphasis of you being an industry expert in your field and include a stronger call to action. The purpose of this video is to assure the viewer that by choosing your company they are making the right choice. This video is meant to get a prospect across the finish line and become one of your customers.

There is no limit to the purpose that videos can be used for. Please reach out and get in touch so we can have a conversation about your needs.
