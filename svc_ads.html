Video Ads

What is a Video Ad?

Running a promotion? This genre of video can be a one off promo or a video that runs every memorial day.

Video ads can be a great way to promote your products and services that your company wants to sell more of. These can be a great asset for your evergreen marketing materials, especially if you run the same sales or promotions every year at the same time, like holidays.

These types of videos find themselves in various stages of your sales funnel. A few different examples could be attracting new customers with an introductory offer, or a video announcing an annual appreciation sale that you have for your loyal customer base. Video ads are extremely versatile and can find themselves on a variety of different platforms to research your intended audience.
